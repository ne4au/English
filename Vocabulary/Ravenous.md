## /ˈræv.ən.əs/
1. #Adjective 
extremely hungry

- I'm ravenous - where's supper?
- Growing boys have ravenous appetites.

##### Synonyms:
- [[Famished]]
- Hungry

2. #Adjective 
wanting something very much or wanting a lot of something

- A ravenous appetite for cash.
- A stage star who was ravenous for attention.
