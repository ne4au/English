## /ˌdet.rəˈmen.t̬əl/   
[[Adj]] [[Formal]]
causing harm or damage

- ==detrimental effect/impact==
These chemicals have a detrimental effect/impact on the environment
- ==detrimental to==
Their decision could be detrimental to the future of the company

##### Synonyms:
- Damaging
- [[Deleterious]]
- Harmful
- Injurious
- [[Prejudicial]]

##### Antonyms:
- Harmless
- [[Innocuous]]
