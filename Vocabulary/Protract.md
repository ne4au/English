## /prəˈtrækt/  
#Verb #Formal 
to make something last for a long time or last longer than necessary

- I have no desire to protract the process.
- Their guest did not protract his stay an hour longer.

##### Synonyms:
- Lengthen
- Prolong
- [[Drag out]]
- [[Draw out]]
- [[Perpetuate]]
