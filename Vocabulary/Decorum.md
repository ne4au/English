## /dɪˈkɔːr.əm/  
[[N]] [[Formal]]
behavior that is controlled, calm, and polite: 

- ==act with decorum==
As young ladies we were expected to act with proper decorum.
- ==behave with decorum==
Employees of this company should behave with decorum and respectability at all times.
