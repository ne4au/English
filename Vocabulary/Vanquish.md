## /ˈvæŋ.kwɪʃ/  
[[V]] [[Literary]]
to defeat an enemy or opponent, especially in war:

- Napoleon was vanquished at the battle of Waterloo in 1815.
- The vanquished army surrendered their weapons

##### Synonyms:
- Annihilate (informal)
- beat (defeat)
- conquer
- crush
- defeat
- [[Thrash]] (defeat)
