## /sɚˈmaɪz/
1. #Verb #Formal
to guess something, without having much of any proof:

- The police surmise (that) the robbers have fled the country

##### Synonyms:
- [[Conjecture]]
- Hypothesize
- Speculate
- Theorize

2. #Noun #Formal
a guess:

- My surmise turned out to be right.
- The article is pure surmise and [[innuendo]].
