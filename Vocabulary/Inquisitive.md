## /ɪnˈkwɪz.ə.t̬ɪv/  
#Adjective 
wanting to discover as much as you can about things, sometimes in a way that annoys people

- An inquisitive child.
- An inquisitive mind.
- She could see inquisitive faces looking out from the windows next door.

##### Synonyms:
- Curious
- Inquiring
- Questioning
- [[Probing]]
- [[Prying]]
- Investigative
- Explorative
