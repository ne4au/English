## /ˈmiː.ɡɚ/  
#Adjective #small_amount

(of amounts of numbers) very small or not enough

- ==meagre salary==
The porters relied on tips, rather than their meagre salary, to earn a living
- ==meagre diet==
The prisoners existed on a meagre diet
