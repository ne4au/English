## /ˈer.ɡoʊ/  
#Adverb  #Formal 
therefore

- He forgot to set his alarm; ergo, he was late for work.
- The store was closed, ergo, we had to find another place to buy groceries.
- She studied [[diligently]] for month; ergo, she aced the exam.

- Therefore
- Thus
- [[Hence]]
- Accordingly
