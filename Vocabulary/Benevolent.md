## /bəˈnev.əl.ənt/
1. #Adjective  
kind and helpful

- He was a benevolent old man and wouldn't hurt a fly.

2. #Adjective 
giving money or help to people or organizations that need it:

- A benevolent organization.

##### Synonyms:
- Kind
- Generous
- Compassionate
- Charitable
- Altruistic
- [[Magnanimous]]
- Philanthropic
