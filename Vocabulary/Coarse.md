## /kɔːrs/  
1. #Adjective  
(ROUGH)
rough and not smooth or soft, or not in very small pieces

- Coarse sand/[[breadcrumbs]].
- ==[[coarse-grained]]==
[[Linen]] is a coarse-grained fabric.

##### Synonyms:
- Rough
- Grainy
- Scratchy
- Bumpy
- Uneven
- [[Harsh]]

2. #Adjective 
(RUDE)
rude and offensive

- A coarse joke.
- Coarse language.
