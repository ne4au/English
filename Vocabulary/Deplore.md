## /dɪˈplɔːr/  
[[V]]
to say or think that something is very bad 

- We deeply deplore the loss of life 
- He said that he deplored all violence 
- ==to be deplored==
The attitude of the Minister is to be deplored (=very bad)

##### Synonyms:
- [[Bemoan]]
- [[Bewail]]
- [[Abhor]]
- [[Denounce]]
- [[Condemn]]
