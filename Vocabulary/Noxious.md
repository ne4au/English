## /ˈnɑːk.ʃəs/  
1. [[Adj]] [[Formal]]
Something, especially a gas or other substance, that is noxious is poisonous or very harmful 

- They died from inhaling noxious fumes

harmful and unpleasant

- a noxious smell/influence
