## /ˈer.ənd/ 
[[N]]
a short journey either to take a message or to take or collect something

- I'll meet you at six, I've got some errands to do/run first

##### Synonyms:
- Task
- Chore
- Job
- Assignment
- Duty
- Mission
- Responsibility
- Duty run
- Day-to-day task
- Outing (when used to imply a small trip for a specific purpose)
