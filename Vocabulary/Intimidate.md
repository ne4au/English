## /ɪnˈtɪm.ə.deɪt/ 
#Verb 
to frighten or threaten someone, usually in order to persuade them to do something that you want them to do:

- They were intimidated into accepting a pay cut by the threat of losing their jobs.

##### Synonyms:
- Frighten
- Bully
- Threaten
- Terrify
- Scare
- [[Daunt]]
- [[Overawe]]
- [[Menace]]
